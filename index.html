ULTRA LIGHTWEIGHT

The Logitech G PRO X SUPERLIGHT is an ultralightweight, wireless gaming mouse. Even though it doesn't have a honeycomb design, it's still among the lightest mice we've ever tested, which is especially impressive considering it's wireless. It offers amazing performance, whether used wired or wirelessly, with exceptionally low click latency, and its CPI can be customized precisely by increments of 50. It's suitable for all hand sizes with any grip type, although the side buttons may be hard to reach with a fingertip grip if you have small hands. You can reprogram all of its buttons, and you can set a G-Shift button to give you a second layer of controls, but this may not be enough buttons for some MMO gamers. This mouse is very similar to the Logitech G Pro Wireless, except the SUPERLIGHT is significantly lighter and only has two side buttons that you can't remove. - rtings.com
